Lasagne

Description

A lasagne is a classic Italian dish made of minced meat between layers of pastry.

Typically, minced beef is used as the filling in a lasagne.

Ingredients

  • Minced beef
  • Pastry
  • Cheese
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Cook the mince
  2. Make a white sauce
  3. Add mince and white sauce in layers between pastry
  4. Cook until ready
